## Article Writing Guidelines:

**Tone & Style:**
- Conversational but authoritative
- New Zealand-focused examples and context
- Click-worthy headlines with intrigue
- Quick, scannable format with bullet points
- Scientific backing but easy to understand
- Call-to-actions for sharing

**Structure for Each:**
- Attention-grabbing introduction
- 3-5 main points with subheadings
- Practical takeaways
- Conclusion with actionable next steps
- Related calculator/workout recommendations

**SEO Keywords to Include:**
- "New Zealand," "NZ," "Kiwi"
- Specific product/brand names
- Location-specific terms (Auckland, Wellington, etc.)
- Comparison terms ("vs," "best," "review")
- Action words ("tested," "revealed," "shocking")

These articles are designed to be highly shareable, tap into curiosity gaps, and provide genuinely useful information that New Zealanders can't find elsewhere. Each article should be 800-1200 words for optimal engagement while remaining quick reads.
